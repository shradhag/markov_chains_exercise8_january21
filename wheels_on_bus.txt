The wheels on the bus go round and round,
round and round,
round and round.
The wheels on the bus go round and round,
all through the town.

The wipers on the bus go Swish, swish, swish;
Swish, swish, swish;
Swish, swish, swish.

all through the town.

Beep, beep, beep;
Beep, beep, beep.

all through the town..

Clink, clink, clink;
Clink, clink, clink.

all through the town.

move on back, move on back;"

all through the town.

Wah, wah, wah".

all through the town.
